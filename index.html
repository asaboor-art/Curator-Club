Our Philosophy

For every taste, every bite, make your dishes colorful and attractive! Use our spices for cooking and naturally marinading meats. Chilies, Spices, Sauces, Dry goods and more! They are our heroes. Make them yours too!

Resume

Shao Foods, Inc. is an import & export trading company, utilizing manufacturers in Chengdu, Chongqing, Beijing, Qingdao, Xiamen, Xingtai, Dezhou and more……., as well as from port cities on the east coast of China and North America. Our rich soil, flat land, and temperate climate provide the perfect environment to grow our spices. We carefully select each and every batch to ensure they will be a great addition to any dish.

 

We employ more than 3000 workers, and are proud to have over 100 food and spice technicians (they know their spices!) to guarantee the best quality and taste, every time. We utilize indoor production and processing warehouses and have numerous chili and spices cold storage facilities, all equipped with advanced equipment and labs. We serve grocery stores, restaurants, and food processing manufacturers in North America, Central and South America.
